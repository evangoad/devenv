devie 0.0.1
usage: devie <commands>
 
commands:
-l, link        symlinks dotfiles to $HOME
-a, archlinux   runs 'pacman' and 'yaourt' scripts.
-b, babun       runs 'pact' scripts.
-c, cygwin      runs 'apt-cyg' and 'choco' scripts.
-o, osx         runs 'brew' and 'brew-cask' scripts.
-u, ubuntu      runs 'apt-get' scripts.
-g, gitenv      create .gitenv in $HOME, git credentials for writing commits.
-r, reload      detect shell, (sh, bash, or zsh) and reload correct dotfile.
